3 steps to enroll using Padeian

1. Admission

This module will facilitate student admission. It will be capable of sorting online and walk-in applicant's details and store admission. The Module will also enable automatic data transfer of successful applicant to Applicant Assessment Desk for fees assessment, mode of payments and other inclusions.

2. Assessment

This module provides for the facilitation of the fees assessment and processing of inclusion in the applicant's rolls.

For Transferees/Irregular College students, The School Administrator may be able to evaluate, choose the applicable subjects for the applicant under his/her course level's curriculum.

3. Payment

This module will support the management and monitoring of the Student's Payment Ledger. It will provide a full history of Liabilities(Tuition and Other Miscellaneous Fees Applicable), Fees Creation for non-Tuition Fees such as Extra-curricular Activities/Field trips etc.
